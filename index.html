ABOUT ASTRA NOVA

Astra Nova Studios is an independent anime studio focused on creating original cinematic stories inspired by powerful emotions, unforgettable worlds and beautiful animation.

Rather than adapting existing manga, our mission is to build completely original anime universes from the ground up.
